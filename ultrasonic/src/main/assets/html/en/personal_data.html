WHAT IS PERSONAL DATA?

Personal data is any information of any nature and regardless of its support, including sound and image, relating to an identified or identifiable person.

Identifiable person is what is considered to be a person who can be identified, directly or indirectly, namely by reference to a name, identification number, location data, identifiers by electronic means (email) or one or more specific elements of your physical identity, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social nature of that person.
